About

An honest conversation about faith.

re:form is a podcast, a journal, and a learning platform — all built around one question: how do we find our way back to God?

Each episode, each journal entry, and each set of study notes is designed to help you engage with the Word of God in a way that is honest, accessible, and life-changing. This is not a platform for people who have it all together. It is for people who are still on the road.
