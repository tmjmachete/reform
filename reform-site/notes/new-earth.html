Last Day Events

The New Earth

← Back to all beliefs

The controversy ends not with loss but with the fullness of what God always intended. We shall be in his presence — no partition, no barrier.

The Final Revelation

Revelation 20:9"But fire came down from heaven and devoured them."
Zechariah 14:9"And the LORD will be king over all the earth. On that day the LORD will be one and his name one."

Satan Unmasked

Great Controversy, p. 498"The discord which his own course had caused in Heaven, Satan charged upon the law and government of God. All evil he declared to be the result of the divine administration... His own work must condemn him. Satan had claimed from the first that he was not in rebellion. The whole universe must see the deceiver unmasked."
John 8:44"He was a murderer from the beginning, and does not stand in the truth, because there is no truth in him."

The Open Door

Luke 15:22–24The robe of righteousness — righteousness by faith — placed on the returning son. All who are willing will heed the call and benefit equally.
Matthew 27:51 & John 19:30"It is finished." The veil of the temple was torn in two. There is no longer any barrier between us and the presence of God.

The only reminder we will carry of what sin has cost will be the marks on the hands of God. And we shall be in his presence — fully, finally, and forever.
