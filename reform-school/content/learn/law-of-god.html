The law of God is not a restriction. It is the expression of God himself — and God is love. To receive the law is to receive love.

What Is the Law?

The fear of God is receiving his wisdom and instruction (Proverbs 1:7). The law is that wisdom — given not to restrict but to liberate. There is no restraint in it, but actual freedom.

Great Controversy, p. 493"The law of love being the foundation of the government of God, the happiness of all created beings depended upon their perfect accord with its great principles of righteousness."

The Sabbath and the Law

Exodus 20:8–10"Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy... for in six days the LORD made heaven and earth."

By keeping the Sabbath we acknowledge God not just as Creator but as Redeemer. Verses 8-9 give the instruction; verse 10 gives the reason — and the reason is God himself.

Love and Obedience

John 14:15–16 (NKJV)"If you love Me, keep My commandments. And I will pray the Father, and He will give you another Helper, that He may abide with you forever."

Obedience is not the opposite of grace. It is the natural response to love. God has made it possible — through the power of the Holy Spirit — for us to live in harmony with his law.
